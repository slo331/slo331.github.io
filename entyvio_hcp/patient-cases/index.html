About Entyvio (vedolizumab)
[Placeholder for local country indications]
Entyvio is indicated for the treatment of:
Ulcerative colitis
Entyvio (vedolizumab) is indicated for the treatment of adult patients with moderately to severely active ulcerative colitis who have had an inadequate response with, have lost response to, or were intolerant to either conventional therapy or a TNFα antagonist.
Crohn’s disease
Entyvio (vedolizumab) is indicated for the treatment of adult patients with moderately to severely active Crohn’s disease who have had an inadequate response with, have lost response to, or were intolerant to either conventional therapy or a TNFα antagonist.
Pouchitis
Entyvio (vedolizumab) is indicated for the treatment of adult patients with moderately to severely active chronic pouchitis who have undergone proctocolectomy and ileal pouch anal anastomosis for ulcerative colitis and have had an inadequate response with, or lost response to, antibiotic therapy.
Intended use
This interactive case has been developed for sales force’s 1:1 interactions with healthcare professionals and for self-navigation by healthcare professionals, subject to local regulations. Local regulations must prevail.
