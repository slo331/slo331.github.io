How it all began...

We started as an in-house careers team of recruitment and creative communications professionals, supporting the international growth of an independent healthcare communications and consultancy agency

After working with several ‘traditional’ recruitment agencies, we saw room for significant improvement in the transactional sales-led services offered to high-growth companies

Developing expertise

From designing and implementing applicant tracking systems to refining our employer branding, our team developed expertise across all areas of the hiring process. We built our reputation on delivering the best candidate experiences and service to our internal clients

We saw first-hand the power of developing sustainable hiring strategies and the value this could bring to growing companies looking to hire exceptional talent

This got us thinking…

What if we could build an agency that utilizes our in-house expertise to support other growing life science businesses?

Introducing Ambit

Ambit was created to provide rapidly-growing life-science companies support that combines sustainable long-term recruitment strategies with the agility to address immediate hiring needs

Our team combines international recruitment experience with cross-sector expertise and specializes in aligning talent with opportunity in the life sciences sector
