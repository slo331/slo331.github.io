Contact a Medical Science Liaison

A medical science liaison (MSL) refers to a field-based medical affairs professional whose primary responsibility is to interact with healthcare providers (HCPs) at the forefront of their relative therapeutic specialties.

MSLs' are able to provide non-promotional, in-depth and balanced scientific information and act as field based experts.
